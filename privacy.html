Privacy Policy

This policy applies to all information collected or submitted on the Dungeon East website and our apps for iPhone, Android, and Windows.

Information we collect

Green Prophet collects anonymous telemetry and diagnostic logging data to help us improve the app. This data is sent to Microsoft Azure Application Insights and may include app usage patterns, error reports, and performance metrics. This data is not linked to your identity and does not include personal information.

iOS users who opt in to provide crash data and usage statistics via the "Share With App Developers" setting are subject to Apple’s Privacy Policy regarding their data, which can be found at https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/en-ww.

Ads and analytics

Green Prophet does not contain or display ads. Anonymous telemetry data is collected via Microsoft Azure Application Insights as described above.

Information usage

Telemetry and logging data is used solely to diagnose issues and improve app quality. We do not sell this data to third parties.

In the future, we may sell to, buy, merge with, or partner with other businesses. As a result of any such transactions, these policies may change.

Your Consent

By using and continuing to use our site or apps, you consent to this privacy policy, as it may change from time to time.
